hong kong (december 2022)

the city has always fascinated me. i made a few hong kong friends in my first year of university, listened to some cantopop and learned very rudimentary cantonese, and off i went to the 'pearl of the orient'.

hong kong was still recovering from the pandemic, and lots of restrictions were still in place, including a three day quarantine period for all visitors where i was restricted to basically wandering around the streets. and just as my luck would have it, the week after i left they announced that pandemic restrictions were ending.
