4.25. Informed Medical Options Party

An anti-vaccination, anti-5G, anti-abortion microparty. I don't think I need to say much.

I know I'm meant to be objective, but sometimes you just can't. Come on. Read on and lose brain cells. You have been warned.

Economy

  • Supports deregulation of the business sector
  • Limit foreign ownership of businesses, land, and water

Foreign policy

  • ‘Investigate the negative legislative impact of Australia's membership of the United Nations’.

Environment

  • Conduct an independent review on the impact of pesticides, insecticides, and herbicides on the environment
  • Phase out ‘all chemicals that affect our environment’

Technology

  • Stop the 5G rollout until ‘independent studies and safety levels are assessed on human and animal health, and the environment’

Medicines

  • Abolish vaccine mandates, including for children and in workplaces, and give people who lose their job due to their lack of vaccination immediate ‘priority’ in the job market
  • Abolish all COVID restrictions
  • Prohibit removal of children from parents who refuse to permit their children to undergo medical treatment
  • End vaccine monitoring by the Therapeutic Goods Administration (T.G.A.) as they have a ‘conflict of interest’
  • Provide legal aid to allow people to sue pharmaceutical companies for ‘vaccine damage’
  • Enforce fines for doctors who do not mention ‘both risks and benefits’ of vaccines
  • Establish a Royal Commission into ‘children damaged or killed as a result of vaccinations’

Family

  • Encourage ‘natural and midwife assisted’ births
  • Increase parental leave to 12 months
  • Supports ‘alternative and home-schooling’

Abortion

  • Ban abortion

Animal welfare

  • End animal testing

Health

  • End flouridation of tap water
  • Include ‘traditional and natural therapies’ in Medicare
  • Leave the World Health Organisation (W.H.O.)

Drug policy

  • Legalise medicinal cannabis
