4.29. Socialist Equality Party (Peter Byrne and Jason Wardle in Victoria)

A minor, non-registered Trotskyist party.

  • Index wages to cost of living
  • Full employment; full-time, permanent jobs to anyone who wants one; provide well-paid apprenticeships
  • Living wage for those unable to work
  • Immediate emergency investment in indigenous communities
  • End exploitation of young people as cheap casual and contract labour
  • Cancel all HECS/student debt
  • Reintroduce COVID measures (contact tracing, isolation, lockdown of areas of high spread)
  • Provide free N95 masks and PCR tests
  • Free, high quality health care and education for all
  • Upgrade and expand access to public transport
  • High-speed internet for all, including remote and rural areas
  • Provide affordable housing for all through the expansion of public housing
  • Establish a national disaster agency to end the use of the military in crises
