4.27. Morgan C Jonas and Monica Smit

Independent Senate candidates for Victoria. Anti-lockdown and anti-vax. In favour of small government, leaving international bodies, etc.

Jonas is a former United Australia Party member and well-known as a ‘freedom’ activist.

Smit is an anti-lockdown activist who was arrested in 2021 and charged with two counts of incitement over her involvement in banned anti-lockdown protests in Melbourne. Is the founder and managing director of Reignite Democracy Australia, an anti-lockdown group opposed to the Andrews ministry.
