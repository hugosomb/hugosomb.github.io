4.10. Great Australian Party, The

Quick profile

Leader: Rod Culleton

Party founded and led by Rodney Culleton, former One Nation senator expelled from the Senate in 2017 due to bankruptcy.

Animal welfare

‘A strong, uniform, properly enforced national legislative framework for the protection of the welfare of animals…’
  • Introduce criminal penalties for mistreatment of animals
  • End use of animals for research and teaching purposes
  • Fund community education programmes on animal needs and care
  • Create independent animal rights regulatory body with prosecution powers
